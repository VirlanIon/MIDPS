This teenager lived alone with his father and the two of them had of very special relationship. Even thought the son was always on the bench his father was always in the stands cheering. 
He never missed a game.  This young man was still the smallest of the class when he entered high school.  


But the young man loved football and decided to hang in there. He was determined to try his best at every practice and perhaps he did  get to play when he became a senior. 
He never missed a practice nor a game but remained a bench warmer all four years of high school. His faithful father was always in the stands always with words of encouragement for him. 
When the young man went to college he decided to try out for the football team as a walk on. His father was sent season tickets for all the college games. This persistent young athlete never missed practice 
during his four years of college but he never got to play in the game.
